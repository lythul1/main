Welcome to Afristudio3

AfriStudio 3 was born out of passion and a love for seamless design and attention to detail. The business began with a focus on Wood Architecture and Kitchens however since then we have ventured into new spaces. Beginning in 2012, we have spent a decade perfecting our expert knowledge with an extensive portfolio which demonstrates our proficiency.

AfriStudio 3 is a business based in South Africa and endeavours to enhance a space with clean cut execution and finishes. Our portfolio spans all over the country, as we firmly believe in travelling where there is a need and opportunity to create customised fitments for the customer.
